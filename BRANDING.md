# CapTrack BE - Branding Guide

## Logo
Located at: `/public/logo.png`

The logo features:
- Mint/teal green upward trending chart
- Clean, modern design on dark background
- Professional fintech aesthetic

## Color Palette

### Primary Color (Mint Green)
- **Hex**: `#4ade80`
- **HSL**: `142 76% 59%`
- **Usage**: Positive values, gains, buy transactions, accents

### Background
- Light mode: White (`#ffffff`)
- Cards: White with subtle shadows

### Text
- Primary: Dark gray/charcoal for high contrast
- Muted: Gray for secondary information

### Accent Colors
- **Gains**: Mint green (`#4ade80`)
- **Losses**: Red (`#dc2626`)
- **Neutral**: Dark foreground

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (clean, modern sans-serif)
- **Financial Numbers**: Monospace, tabular nums for alignment

## Component Styling

### Cards
- Clean white cards with subtle borders
- Border color uses mint green for active/important cards
- Shadow: Soft, minimal

### Buttons
- Primary: Mint green background, white text
- Outline: Mint green border
- Ghost: Transparent with mint hover

### Data Display
- Numbers use tabular-nums for clean alignment
- Currency formatted in Belgian locale (â‚¬)
- Dates in DD/MM/YYYY format

## Design Principles
1. **Clean & Professional**: Minimal clutter, focus on data
2. **Trust & Security**: Privacy-first messaging prominent
3. **Clarity**: Clear hierarchy, easy-to-read financial data
4. **Modern Fintech**: Contemporary design aligned with logo
